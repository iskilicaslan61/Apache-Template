Why We Use AWS

SpeedCycle leverages Amazon Web Services (AWS) to ensure high availability, secure hosting, and scalable infrastructure. Here’s what we use:

  • Amazon EC2 – To host our website and backend services.
  • Amazon S3 – For secure and scalable image and data storage.
  • Amazon RDS – For our reliable product and customer databases.
  • CloudFront – For content delivery across the globe.

Using AWS allows us to focus on bikes and customer service, while AWS takes care of speed, uptime, and security.
